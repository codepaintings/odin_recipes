Ramen

Description

Ramen is a very nutrious Japanese dish

It is a soup and can be made suitable for vegetarians with a multitude of ingredients

Ingredients

  • Veg stock
  • noodles
  • Egg
  • Beansprouts
  • Kale
  • Sesame

Steps

  1. Prepare the special Japanese egg
  2. Chop the ingredients such as kale
  3. Prepare the broth inc the veg stock
  4. Add the noodles to the broth for cooking
  5. Add all the ingredients to the dish
  6. And of course... enjoy!
