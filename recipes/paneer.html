Paneer

How to make the paneer

It is important to try and buy real legitimate tandoori paneer.

Ideally this means paneer in a tandoori oven, but recently I have also don it successfully in an air fryer.

Ingredients

  • Paneer cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Paprila
  • Cumin
  • Coconut milk
  • rice

Steps

  1. Cut the paneer
  2. Fry the paneer
  3. Fry the onions with spices
  4. Add the paneer and onion with cocnut milk
  5. Cook the rice
  6. Add the rice to the spices
  7. Enjoy
